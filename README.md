# 🌾 AgriGuard AI

AgriGuard AI is a smart farming platform designed to help farmers detect crop diseases, predict pest risks, monitor soil health, and receive intelligent farming recommendations.

The platform combines crop analysis, weather insights, soil monitoring, and market intelligence into one simple and farmer friendly system.

## 🚀 Features

### 🔍 Disease Detection
- Detect crop diseases using image upload
- Identify severity levels:
  - Low
  - Medium
  - High

### 🌦 Pest Risk Prediction
- Predict pest outbreaks using weather and humidity data
- Provide early risk alerts

### 💊 Smart Recommendations
- Suggest suitable medicines and pesticides
- Provide dosage and usage instructions
- Prevention and crop care guidance

### 💧 Irrigation Suggestions
- Recommend when and how much water to apply
- Based on soil moisture analysis

### 🌱 Soil pH Analysis
- Detect acidic, neutral, or alkaline soil
- Suggest treatments to maintain soil balance

### 📍 Location Based Advisory
- Provide recommendations based on:
  - Weather
  - Soil conditions
  - Crop data
  - Farmer location

### 📊 Risk Level Dashboard
- Green = Safe
- Yellow = Medium Risk
- Red = High Risk

### 🌐 Language Support
- English and Hindi translation support

### 🌙 Dark/Light Mode
- User-friendly theme switching

### 📈 Market Insights
- High demand crops
- Crop prices
- Import/export trends
- Profitable crop suggestions
